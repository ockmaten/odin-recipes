T's Chili

Recipe Description

T doesn't really measure anything. He cooks with this heart on that one! But here is what he did today, as I'm typing his recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 carrots
  • 3 stalks of celery
  • 2 onions
  • chili spices (1 package)
  • 2 cans of beans (pinto, red, kidney
  • vegetable juice

Toppings

  • grated cheddar cheese or any cheese
  • sour cream
  • cheez-itz
  • nutritional yeast
  • red onions

Steps

  1. chop all the vegetables and cook them in olive oil
  2. cook them down and add a little bit of beer or apple cider vinegar until it reduces
  3. add the spices and stir
  4. add the beans and vegetable juice
  5. add salt to taste and a bay leaf
  6. let it simmer for a least 30 minutes, but the longer the better
