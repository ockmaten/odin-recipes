Pain de Thon

Recipe Description

The best tuna loaf in the world!

Ingredients

  • 4 or 5 beaten eggs
  • 1 large can of natural tuna
  • 20 cl of creme fraiche
  • 1 small can of tomato concentrate
  • 1 teapsoon of flour
  • salt
  • pepper
  • shallots
  • 4 or 5 sprigs of chopped parsley

Steps

  1. mix everthing and cook in a bain marie in a loaf pan for about 40 minutes
